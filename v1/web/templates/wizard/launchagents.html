Four LaunchAgents auto-start on login: relay (your chosen transport), scheduler (briefs + reminders + email watch + delivery watch + expected arrivals + retention purges), log-rotation (daily at 03:00), and webui (this very page at 127.0.0.1:8780).

Without this step:

  • The relay won't be listening for incoming messages.
  • The morning brief won't fire.
  • You'd have to manually launch each daemon every time you reboot.

Click below to run ./launch_agents/install.sh — renders the four plists with absolute paths to your venv's Python and loads them via launchctl bootstrap. Idempotent; safe to re-run.
