Email triage is configured in config/triggers.yaml. The defaults work for most people. Edit any time at /config/triggers — the scheduler re-reads on every tick, no restart needed.

What's on by default:

  • Real-time email triage (Haiku decides whether each non-automated unread email is worth a ping)
  • Morning brief at 07:30 local — includes today's calendar, top tasks, urgent unread emails
  • Sunday weekly review at 20:00 local
  • Delivery watch (UPS / FedEx / Amazon / USPS / DHL)

Privacy opt-out:

Set EMAIL_TRIAGE_LOCAL_ONLY=true in .env to stop sending any email content to Anthropic. You won't get email pings; the rest of the brief still works.
